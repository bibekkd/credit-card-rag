1. Axis Atlas Credit Card
axis atlas credit card

Joining Fee: Rs. 5,000
Annual Fee: Rs. 5,000

Key Features and Benefits:

Welcome benefit of up to 2,500 bonus EDGE Miles
5 EDGE Miles per Rs. 100 spent on monthly travel spends of up to Rs. 2 Lakh
2 EDGE Miles per Rs. 100 spent on monthly travel spends above Rs. 2 Lakh
2 EDGE Miles per Rs. 100 spent across all categories
Redeem EDGE Miles through the Travel EDGE platform at a ratio of 1:1 (1 EDGE Mile= Rs. 1)
Value of 1 EDGE Mile = 2 Partner Points; except for 2 EDGE Miles = 1 Marriott Bonvoy Point
Up to 12 international lounge access in a year*
Up to 18 domestic lounge access in a year*
Milestone benefit of up to 5,000 EDGE Miles*
*Subject to annual spends.

Conclusion:

Axis Atlas credit card is an excellent option for those who spend a substantial amount every month on travel as well as other expenses. The card offers 10% value-back on travel spends as reward points redeemable across 15+ airlines and hotels in 1:2 ratio. On spends beyond travel, users can get up to 4% value-back as 2 points per Rs. 100. Along with the highest conversion ratio, the card has a unique tier-based structure allowing high-spenders to extract maximum value from the card by reaching higher tiers with better benefits. However, being a travel credit card, the only disappointing bit is that this card charges a high forex mark-up fee of 3.5% and offers no concierge services.

HSBC TravelOne Credit Card

Joining Fee: Rs. 4,999
Annual Fee: Rs. 4,999

Key Features and Benefits:

4 reward points per Rs. 100 spent on flights, travel aggregators, and in foreign currency
2 reward points per Rs. 100 spent on other categories
6 complimentary domestic lounge visits every year
4 complimentary international lounge access per year
Exclusive discount offers on travel platforms like EaseMyTrip, Yatra, ClearTrip, etc.

Conclusion: 

HSBC TravelOne Credit Card is an airline-agnostic travel credit card that offers value back in the form of reward points. Cardholders can earn 4% value-back on travel spends through accelerated (2X) rewards points and redeem them with partner airlines and hotels at a 1:1 ratio, allowing them to save on their travel expenses. The card also provides exclusive discount offers on travel platforms like Yatra, EaseMyTrip, Cleartrip, PayTM, and StayVista Hotels, along with complimentary lounge access, which further enhances the value of this card for frequent travellers. Besides travel, TravelOne also offers lifestyle benefits such as complimentary golf rounds, dining discount, and more.
